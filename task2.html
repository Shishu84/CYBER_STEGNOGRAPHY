What is Generative AI?

Generative AI Overview

Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that can create new content, such as text, images, music, or even videos. It learns patterns from existing data and uses that knowledge to generate realistic and useful outputs.

Generative AI is changing the way we work, learn, and create. It is used in education, design, entertainment, and many other industries. While it brings many benefits, it is also important to use it responsibly.
